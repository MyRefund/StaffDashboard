Get paid out within 3–4 working days

Most donation tax credit claims we file are paid out within 3–4 working days.

Claiming yourself can take up to 12 weeks with Inland Revenue. We take care of the paperwork, lodge everything correctly, and help avoid delays.

Choose to give your refund back

You can choose to have your donation tax refund paid directly back to the charity instead of to you.

We work directly with the charity on your behalf. They provide the donation receipts, we prepare and file your claim, and once Inland Revenue pays the refund, we pass it back to the charity.

  • We liaise directly with the charity
  • Receipts are collected and verified
  • We file your claim with Inland Revenue
  • Your refund is paid back to the charity

How it works

1.

Share your donation details

Send us your donation receipts, or let your charity provide them to us on your behalf.

2.

We file your claim

We prepare and lodge your donation tax credit claim with Inland Revenue for you.

3.

Receive your refund

Once processed, your refund is paid either to you or back to the charity if you choose.
