Income Confirmation Script

Opening

We’re just going to quickly run through your 202X tax year, which covers 1 April 202X through to 31 March 202X.

I’ve got your income summary from Inland Revenue here, so I’ll read through what they have recorded and you can let me know if everything sounds right.

Read income summary from IRD.
Does that all sound correct to you?
Pause and confirm. If earnings are under $5,000, complete MID questions as required.

Great.

Additional Income Check

Now I just want to make sure there’s nothing else we need to include before we file your return.

Since 1 April 202X, have you earned any other income that wasn’t already taxed automatically?

That could include things like:

  • Working for yourself or doing contract work
  • Earning money through Uber, rideshare, or delivery apps
  • Rental income or selling a property within two years
  • Overseas income
  • Income from a company, partnership, or trust
  • Investment income like interest, shares or Māori Authority Distributions
  • Anything else outside of normal wages or salary
Have you had anything like that?

And finally, do you have any expenses or charitable donations you’d like us to include?

Pause and confirm responses.

Final Confirmation & Declaration

Just to confirm before we file:
  • We’ve reviewed the income information held by IRD.
  • You’ve told us about any additional income or expenses.
  • We’ll prepare and file your return based on this information and what we’ve sourced from Inland Revenue.
  • If there’s any tax to pay, that will need to be paid directly to IRD by the due date.
  • By confirming now, you’re agreeing to My Refund’s Terms and Conditions and confirming that everything discussed is true and correct. This will act as your electronic signature.
Are you happy to confirm that?
