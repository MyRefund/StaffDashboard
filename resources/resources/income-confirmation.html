Income Confirmation Script

Opening Statement

All this information we are about to go through is for the 202X tax year, which runs from 1 April 202X through to 31 March 202X.

Earnings Confirmation

Read through IRD earnings and get the client to confirm all details are correct.

If earnings information is under $5,000 — ask MID questions.

Additional Income Questions

  • Did you receive any untaxed income?
  • Any interest, dividends or Māori Authority distributions over $200?
  • Are you part of a trust or family trust?
  • Have you received any overseas income?
  • Have you arrived, or been overseas for more than 183 days?
  • Have you been self-employed, or done any work for a rideshare or food delivery company?
  • Do you have any personal or business expenses to claim?
  • Do you own a company and earn income from a business, partnership or shareholder income?
  • Have you sold a property within 2 years of purchase, or received any rental income?
  • Have you received income from an Employee Share Scheme?
  • Have you made any donations to a charitable organisation over $5?

Declaration

  • We have reviewed the earnings information, including employer name, months listed and gross income / PAYE.
  • We have reviewed any investment income details including provider, months received and gross income / tax.
  • You have advised My Refund Ltd of any additional income and/or expenses not listed on the earnings summary.
  • My Refund Ltd will sign and file your tax return based on information provided or sourced from Inland Revenue.
  • You will pay any tax owing to IRD on or before the due date and do not hold My Refund Ltd liable for any resulting tax bill.
  • You have read and agree to our terms and conditions.

Final Confirmation

Please confirm all information discussed is true and correct, and that your agreement to this declaration constitutes your electronic signature.
